How do I mint a Voidwalker?

What are the holder benefits?
Holders gain access to the Void DAO, exclusive events, future airdrops, and first access to all Season 2 mints.
Is the art fully on-chain?
Yes. All metadata and generative attributes are stored on-chain, ensuring your Voidwalker exists permanently regardless of any third-party service.

5% royalties on secondary sales. 60% goes to the community treasury, 40% to the core development team for continued builds.
